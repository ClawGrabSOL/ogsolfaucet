VI · Frequently Asked

The details.

Is SPCX equity?

No. SPCX is an on-chain digital asset and does not represent ownership of any company, fund, security, or future profit interest. It is a participation token native to the Solana network.

How are allocations determined?

Through faucet participation and snapshot ownership. Every verified wallet that claims through the faucet is recorded. At launch, balances are snapshotted and transitioned into the live circulating supply proportional to ownership.

Which wallets are supported?

Phantom and Solflare on Solana mainnet. You may also paste a wallet address directly into the faucet panel.

When does activation occur?

At the launch countdown expiration. The snapshot is captured at that moment, and circulating supply distribution is finalized on-chain immediately after.

Are there private rounds or insider allocations?

No. There are no private rounds, no VC allocations, no team unlocks, and no insider rights. Supply enters circulation exclusively through the faucet.

Can I claim multiple times?

One claim per wallet address. Duplicate attempts are rejected by the protocol.
